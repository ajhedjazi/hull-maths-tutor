About Citadel Fitness

Citadel Fitness is personal training for people who want structure, accountability, and a realistic plan that fits around normal life. The focus is simple: build strength, improve confidence, and create habits that last.

About me

I coach people who want clear training, steady support, and honest accountability. Whether you train in person or online, I help you put structure around your week, build confidence with the basics, and stay consistent when life gets busy.
